about me

hey there! i'm nikki.

I'm a recent Computer Science graduate from Williams College with a passion for designing thoughtful, user-centered experiences. I was drawn to UX/UI design because it combines creativity with intention—every interface choice matters!

I have over a year of hands-on UX/UI experience and currently serve as Lead UX/UI Designer and Co-Engineer at an early-stage startup, where I'm building a 0→1 platform to bridge differences through structured, facilitated online conversations.

I'm always looking to grow as a designer, exploring ways to make interfaces intuitive, accessible, and enjoyable for every user.
